Great clinical care deserves an equally great patient access experience.

Before patients can benefit from outstanding clinical care, they first need to find your practice, receive timely responses, and schedule an appointment. For many psychiatry practices, improving patient access is one of the greatest opportunities to better connect patients with the care they need.

Common patient access challenges include:

  • Heavy reliance on referrals or Psychology Today
  • Limited visibility on Google and emerging AI search
  • Missed calls and delayed responses
  • Prospective patients abandoning the scheduling process
  • Administrative work consuming valuable clinical time
  • Uncertainty about how to market ethically and professionally
