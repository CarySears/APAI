Common Patient Access Challenges

Challenges That Limit Practice Growth

  • Overreliance on referrals, Psychology Today, and other directories for new patients
  • Limited visibility in Google search
  • Limited visibility in AI search and AI-generated answers, including Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and similar platforms
  • An unclaimed or poorly optimized Google Business Profile
  • A website that is not attracting enough qualified prospective patients
  • Uncertainty about how to market ethically and professionally

Challenges That Create Administrative Burden

  • Missed calls and delayed responses
  • Prospective patients struggling to get answers
  • Confusing or difficult appointment booking
  • Prospective patients abandoning the scheduling process
  • Administrative work consuming valuable clinical time
  • Website accessibility issues that create barriers to care
